I am a senior researcher at Oracle Labs, Australia. My research interest includes deep learning, graph neural networks, and domain applications such as natural language processing and security. I received my PhD from the <a href="https://www.monash.edu/it/dsai">Department of Data Science and AI</a> at <a href="https://www.monash.edu/">Monash University</a>, Australia, in May 2021, under the supervision of <a href="http://dinhphung.ml">Dinh Phung</a>, <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=4hT6E04AAAAJ&hl=en">Tu Dinh Nguyen</a>, and Geoff Webb. Before that, I obtained my MSc from <a href="https://www.uni-saarland.de/en/home.html">Saarland University</a>, Germany and my BSc from <a href="https://e.uet.vnu.edu.vn">VNU University of Engineering and Technology</a>, Vietnam.
